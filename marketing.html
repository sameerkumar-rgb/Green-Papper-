04 / FAQ

Marketing team questions,
answered.

How does the launch calendar sync work?

The launch calendar pulls directly from the product roadmap. When engineering moves a ship date, your campaign timeline updates automatically — no manual tracking.

Can we create content briefs from product specs?

Yes. Green Papper auto-generates content briefs from product specs, including target persona, value props, and technical details — ready for blog posts, emails, and landing pages.

Does it integrate with our marketing stack?

Yes. We connect with HubSpot, Marketo, Google Analytics, Webflow, Mixpanel, Intercom, Contentful, and more. Your campaigns stay in your tools, with added product context.

How do approval workflows work?

Route messaging, positioning, and assets through structured approvals. Legal, brand, and product teams sign off in one flow — no more scattered email threads.

Can we track campaign performance?

Yes. Campaign analytics tie marketing metrics back to product adoption, activation, and retention — so you can prove the revenue impact of every launch.

Do we need to learn a new tool?

Green Papper's marketing features are designed to feel familiar. Most teams are productive within a day, and launch alerts come through Slack where you already work.
